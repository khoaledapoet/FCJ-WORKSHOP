Event 1

Event Report: “FCAJ Community Day - Conference Call”

Purpose of the Event

  • To share deep technical knowledge on Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence (AI/GenAI) from industry experts.
  • To provide guidance on building knowledge management systems (Second Brain) and smart data analytics.
  • To conduct deep dives into Content Delivery Networks (CloudFront) and the operational mechanics of Large Language Models (LLMs).
  • To network and learn from real-world implementation case studies, specifically a hackathon experience from VIB Bank engineers.

List of Speakers

  • Anh Tịnh - Topic: Build second brain
  • Hải Anh - Topic: Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon QuickSight
  • Thịnh - Topic: From Edge To Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation
  • Team VIB - Topic: 36 hrs with LotusHacks – Building UTMorpho from ideal to Reality
  • Đào Đức - Topic: Deep dive talk: How LLM actually works?
  • Cát Vy - Topic: Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System

Key Highlights

Cloud Architecture & Content Delivery

  • From Edge to Origin (Speaker: Thịnh): An in-depth analysis of the AWS CloudFront CDN. The session detailed the packet journey from Edge Locations to Origin servers, optimal caching strategies, and transit security.
  • Knowledge Management (Speaker: Anh Tịnh): Methodologies for building a “Second Brain” to systematically organize and retain technological knowledge.

AI, LLMs & Multi-Agent Systems

  • The Core of LLMs (Speaker: Đào Đức): A deep dive into how Large Language Models operate “under the hood,” decoding non-determinism and parameter control for GenAI outputs.
  • Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent (Speaker: Cát Vy): Architectural blueprints for building enterprise-level Multi-Agent AI systems, where various AI models collaborate to solve complex workflows.
  • AI Assistants with QuickSight (Speaker: Hải Anh): Integrating AI capabilities into Amazon QuickSight to analyze business data and generate visual charts through user-friendly interfaces.

Real-World Case Study

  • 36 Hours with LotusHacks (Team VIB): VIB’s engineering team shared their hands-on hackathon experience, demonstrating how to build rapid solutions under extreme time pressure while adhering to strict financial compliance standards.

Lessons Learned

Architectural Mindset

  • Edge Optimization: Mastered the philosophy of pushing data delivery and security perimeters as close to the end-users as possible via CloudFront, reducing the load on core backend servers.
  • Systematic AI Integration: Instead of using AI as a standalone tool, I learned to adopt a systematic mindset through Multi-Agent architectures, allowing automated AI workflows to communicate and solve macro-level problems.

Technical Capabilities

  • Data Analytics & BI: Understood the analytical power of Amazon QuickSight augmented with AI, enabling natural language data querying instead of writing complex SQL commands.
  • AI/LLM Mechanics: Grasped the mathematical and probabilistic nature of LLMs, empowering me to execute effective prompt tuning and mitigate AI hallucination.

Application to Work

  • PawVerse Architecture Optimization: Directly applied insights from Thịnh’s presentation to configure AWS CloudFront for the project’s Edge Layer, effectively resolving CORS errors and accelerating S3 static asset delivery.
  • Project Documentation: Applied the “Second Brain” methodology from Anh Tịnh to systematically organize my source code, infrastructure diagrams, and Workshop documentation.
  • AI in Operations: Leveraged the LLM deep-dive knowledge (shared by Đào Đức) to accurately control and prompt the AWS Kiro assistant while writing automated infrastructure scripts.

Event Experience

Attending the AWS Vietnam Community Day was a fantastic experience that connected me with a passionate community of Cloud and AI enthusiasts.

Learning from Top-Tier Experts

  • The speakers were seasoned, hands-on professionals. Listening to “Deep dive” analyses on LLMs and CloudFront architectures provided a technical depth that standard theoretical documentation rarely conveys.

Practical Technical Insights

  • I was particularly impressed by the Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent architecture and how Team VIB engineered a product in just 36 hours. This heavily inspired me regarding deployment velocity and working under pressure.

Key Takeaways

  • Cloud Computing and AI are converging rapidly. As a network/system engineer, merely knowing about infrastructure (VPC, EC2) is no longer sufficient; integrating AI solutions and understanding LLM mechanics will be vital survival skills in the future.
